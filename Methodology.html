How we created these

  1. Through shape and size

    2. This was created by taking data from the ArcGIS living atlas of Ontario and then symbolizing it differently by using 2 fields instead of 1. We used the total ballots cast and the party won this now shows the map through the number of people who voted instead of through the area.

  2. Map dashboard

    3. We created this by getting our data from Open Canada/Canadian government and geo-referencing the data to locate it on ArcGIS Pro. Once the dashboard was created we added an indicator, a gauge, a list, and a pie graph. we then made sure it changed off of zoom and/or select. This made it easy for people to see exact information from exact electoral districts.
